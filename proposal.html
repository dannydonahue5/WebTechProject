Project Proposal Questions:

    • What is your website about?
      •

      My website is going to be dedicated to teaching yourself how to play guitar.

    • What is the objective of your website?
      •

      The objective of my website is to show my users that you can teach yourself anything, at any age, with persistence and passion. When I was given a lot of free time during COVID, I spent it teaching myself guitar. There are a lot of things I learned and advice I have now about how I would teach myself if I were to do it over again.

    • Who is your target audience?
      •

      My target audience is anybody who wants to learn an instrument. I don't think this project has an age focus, but I can market this best towards college-aged individuals using my networks.

    • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
      •

      I have a few ideas of things that I want to include. First I want to have an about me/my story page on the website. Here, I will talk about how I learned guitar, my struggles, and include old videos of me vs. videos of me playing now. Next, I want to divide my website into a beginner section (with advice, information, and song recommendations with examples) and an intermediate section (with the same content). I also want to include sections about choosing which guitars to purchase and reviews on each (something I want to do anyway), and maybe advice on how to record (this is something I want to get more into but have to do more research about.) I have tons of videos of me playing already (I know 50+ songs and have 30+ well recorded videos), so I won't be doing a ton of extra outside work on that front. I also want to include a sample practice schedule and videos of scales/exercises to practice.
