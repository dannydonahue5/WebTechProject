Advice Reasoning
Learn the beginner chords first They make up the majority of songs you will first learn
Start with easy songs that you like Makes you more motivated to practice and master it
Practice Everyday Picking up the guitar for a little bit everyday will make a huge difference
Never put your guitar in a case (unless your traveling) I play whenever I need a break from work, putting it in a case makes this much harder
Be realistic with your goals What do you want out of guitar?
