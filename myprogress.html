November 2020: "Wonderful Tonight"

This video was taken about 3 months into learning. It was the first song that I learned how to play in full. This is a great song for beginners because it builds on all of the beginner chords you first learn. As you can hear, my playing lacked the flow that makes the song great. However, at the time I recorded the video I was very proud of how far I came in a short time.

December 2020: "Blackbird"

Recorded soon after, I learned my second song. This song is fun to learn because you use the entirety of the guitar, and it is not particularly difficult. I am glad I stopped making that face when I play.

April 2021: "Here Comes the Sun"

Now at about 8 months into learning, I can see noticeable improvement. In this song I am still building off of the basic chords but I am adding in different techniques which I will build on in the upcoming videos. At this point in my learning I was really happy about how I was beginning to sound, especially because I didn't feel like I was annoying people by practicing.

January 2022: "Queen of California"

I surprisingly do not have many videos from 2021 - I went through a phase of not recording because I felt that it was taking away from the joy I got from playing. I realized the importance of recording because it creates a cool log of progress - I try to dedicate about one day a week to recording new songs. That being said, this was a song I really enjoyed playing.

February 2022: Wonderful Tonight (pt. II)

About one year and a half in, I recorded this harder version of a song I already learned "Wonderful Tonight." I use more intermediate/advanced techniques here. There is a great difference between my 2020 version and my 2022 version of the song - this was a point that I realized I had come a long way.

March 2022: "Fly me to the Moon"

Honestly, when I started one of my goals had been to play this song at regular speed. It was after recording this video when I realized truly I could learn any song that I wanted to as long as I put the time in and broke it down.

October 2022: "Age of Worry"

This video was recorded very recently, it has an interesting picking pattern that was tricky to get down but it is very fun to play. Now, I can take song suggestions and I will be expanding into electric guitar.
